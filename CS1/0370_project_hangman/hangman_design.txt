
For 35 points, turn in a design for a game of hangman.
Your design must include the following.

1. List the steps of a game of Hangman in order.
How is the game begun? How is it played? How does the game end?
Describe each step.

2. Which of the actions or steps you listed should be functions?
List the functions you expect to use. Name each function and say what
it will do. The functions should be small actions.

3. What information will you need to store to play a game of hangman?
List the information needed and name a variable for each piece of
information. You do not need to give the type of these variables.
