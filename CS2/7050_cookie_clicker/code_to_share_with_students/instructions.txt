
For 140 points, write a game of cookie clicker including:
	-displaying a cookie image and a count of how many cookies have been earned.
	-the ability to click on the cookie to increase the cookie count.
	-displaying at least 3 upgrades such as cursor, mine, and farm.
	-the ability to click on the upgrades to increase their level.
	-earning cookies over time based on the level of the mine and farm.
	-earning more cookies per click based on the level of the cursor.

The provided code will help you get started by showing you
	-how to detect mouse clicks
	-how to write text on the screen

If you struggle with how to detect which button was clicked, check out the mouseover examples in the next folder.
